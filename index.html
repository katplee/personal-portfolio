Short description
On a quest to become the greatest warrior ever, the main hero tames and fuses with dragons to become stronger. A simple RPG with turn-based fighting with enemies in-game!
Tasks
Joined a team from South America and fought through a 14-hour time difference to create a playable RPG game for the Brackey's Game Jam 2021.1 where I wrote almost 95% of the code. Built the systems for all game elements, implemented player movement, created the inventory system with scriptable objects, scripted the whole turn-based combat system, designed the save and load system between scenes using serialization and implemented the update system for the HUD and panels for all scenes.

Hi! Call me Kat.
I have been independently developing games for just a little over a year now. What started out as a hobby quickly turned to passion and I have been going at it non-stop ever since. I am self-taught and have experienced developing games solo and with a team where online documentations and tutorials were my ultimate go-tos.

I am currently working as a mechanical engineer focusing on mechanical design and measurement accuracy improvement, experiences which have helped me with the technical side of programming. I am keen on utilizing my engineering background to gain a footing in the game development industry where I look forward to learning about the complete game development process as well as the structure and systems behind implementation of multiplayer games and big titles, both 2D and 3D.

I live for the grind of looking for a solution to a problem at hand and enjoy the fulfillment of solving it afterwards. The belief that games have the capacity to put the spotlight on important matters and questionable cultures and traditions and, consequently, bring about change (the good kind!) vital to the improvement of human life has given me the drive to do what I do.
